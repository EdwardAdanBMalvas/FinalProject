Job Impact

Job Creation

Aside from taking jobs, it also allowed the creation of new jobs. These new jobs can be seen especially in Cybersecurity, Data Analysis, Digital marketing, and many more.

Skill Demand

Many jobs today require different skills from jobs in the past. Technology allowed manual labor to turn into automation which gives people a chance to work on new skills fit for the changing society.

Flexibility

Thanks to technology, work can be done from anywhere, allowing global talent to be used globally.
